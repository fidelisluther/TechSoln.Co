Recent projects

NexHub: A media company website

Tech Soln Co. created a modern and responsive website for NexHub, a media company that specializes in creating engaging and informative content. The website features a clean and intuitive design, easy navigation, and optimized content to improve the user experience. Our team also implemented a custom CMS to enable the client to easily manage their content and update their website.


Terrisa Foundation: A charity organization website

Tech Soln Co. developed a website for Terrisa Foundation, a charity organization that focuses on providing support to underprivileged children. The website features a user-friendly interface, clear calls-to-action, and engaging visuals to encourage visitors to take action and support the organization's cause. Our team also integrated a donation system to enable the foundation to receive donations online and track donor activity.
